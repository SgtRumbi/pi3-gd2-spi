Connect the Gameduino 3 to a Raspberry Pi 3 via the onboard
SPI.

+-------------------------------+
| Connect (Pi3)    | With (GD2) |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 2 (or 4)/+5V | +5V        |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 6/GND        | GND        |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 19 (MOSI)    | MOSI       |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 21 (MISO)    | MISO       |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 23 (SCK)     | SCK        |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 24 (CE0)     | GPU sel    |
|------------------+------------|
| Pin 26 (CE1)     | SD sel     |
+-------------------------------+
